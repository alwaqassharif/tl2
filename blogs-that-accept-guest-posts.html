Blogs That Accept Guest Posts: A Complete Guide to Getting Published

Are you struggling to grow your website traffic, build backlinks, or get noticed online? You’re not alone. Many beginners and even experienced marketers find it difficult to identify blogs that accept guest posts and successfully get their content published.

The good news is that guest posting is still one of the most powerful and safe SEO strategies when done correctly. In this guide, you’ll learn how to find blogs accepting guest posts, how to pitch them, and how to create content that gets approved—explained in a simple, practical, and human-friendly way.

Image Example of Guest Posting Workflow

What Are Blogs That Accept Guest Posts?

Blogs that accept guest posts are websites that allow writers, marketers, or businesses to publish articles on their platform. In return, you usually get a backlink, exposure, and authority in your niche.

These are often called:

  • Blogs accepting guest posts
  • Guest post accepting blogs
  • Write for us blogs

Why Do Blogs Accept Guest Posts?

  • They need fresh and valuable content regularly
  • It helps them reach new audiences
  • They build relationships with other experts
  • It improves their SEO and content diversity

Benefits of Guest Posting

Submitting content to marketing blogs that accept guest posts offers multiple benefits:

  • High-quality backlinks: Improve your domain authority and rankings
  • Targeted traffic: Get visitors interested in your niche
  • Brand awareness: Build trust and authority
  • Networking: Connect with industry experts

How to Find Blogs Accepting Guest Posts

Finding the right blogs is the first step. Here are proven methods:

1. Use Google Search Operators

  • "write for us" + your niche
  • "guest post" + your topic
  • "blogs that accept guest posts" + niche
  • "submit guest post" + keyword

2. Analyze Competitor Backlinks

Use SEO tools like Ahrefs or SEMrush to check where your competitors are getting backlinks. Many of those sites are guest post accepting blogs.

3. Use Social Media Platforms

Search on LinkedIn, Twitter, or Facebook groups where bloggers often share guest posting opportunities.

4. Join Blogging Communities

  • Reddit (SEO and blogging subreddits)
  • Facebook groups
  • Online forums

How to Choose the Right Blogs

Not every blog is worth your effort. Here’s what to check before submitting:

  • Domain Authority (DA): Aim for DA 30+
  • Relevance: Blog should match your niche
  • Traffic: Check if the blog has real visitors
  • Content quality: Avoid spammy or low-quality sites

How to Pitch Guest Posts Successfully

Finding blogs that accept guest posts is only half the job. You also need to pitch effectively.

1. Personalize Your Email

Always address the blog owner by name and mention their recent content.

2. Suggest Unique Topics

  • Offer 2–3 topic ideas
  • Make sure they are not already published
  • Focus on solving a real problem

3. Keep It Short and Clear

Blog owners receive many emails daily, so keep your pitch simple and direct.

4. Show Your Expertise

Include links to your previous work or portfolio to build trust.

How to Write a Guest Post That Gets Approved

Even if a blog accepts guest posts, your content must meet quality standards.

1. Follow Guidelines Carefully

Every blog has its own rules—word count, format, linking, etc.

2. Write for the Audience

Focus on helping readers, not just promoting yourself.

3. Use Simple and Clear Language

Avoid jargon and keep sentences easy to understand.

4. Add Value

  • Provide actionable tips
  • Use examples
  • Include real insights

5. Optimize for SEO

  • Use the main keyword naturally
  • Add related keywords like "blogs accepting guest posts"
  • Use proper headings

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Sending generic emails
  • Choosing low-quality blogs
  • Over-optimizing anchor text
  • Writing promotional content
  • Ignoring blog guidelines

Tips to Scale Guest Posting

  • Create a list of 50+ blogs accepting guest posts
  • Send 5–10 outreach emails daily
  • Track responses using a spreadsheet
  • Reuse content ideas across different blogs
  • Build long-term relationships with editors

FAQs About Blogs That Accept Guest Posts

1. Are guest posts still effective for SEO?

Yes, guest posting remains one of the safest and most effective ways to build high-quality backlinks and improve rankings.

2. How many guest posts should I do per month?

Start with 4–8 high-quality guest posts per month instead of focusing on quantity.

3. Do all blogs allow backlinks?

No, some blogs provide nofollow links, but they still offer traffic and exposure benefits.

4. Can beginners submit guest posts?

Yes, beginners can start with small blogs and gradually move to high-authority sites.

5. Is guest posting free or paid?

It depends. Some blogs accept free guest posts, while others charge a publishing fee.

Conclusion

Finding and publishing on blogs that accept guest posts is one of the smartest ways to grow your online presence, build backlinks, and establish authority. The key is to focus on quality, relevance, and genuine value.

Start by identifying the right guest post accepting blogs, craft personalized pitches, and create helpful content that solves real problems. With consistency and the right strategy, you’ll see long-term SEO and branding benefits.
